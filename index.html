For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add a brown sugar & stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs & toss in the saucepan with water & lemon juice. cook over medium heat, stiring frequently, until you have a chunky jmmy mixture. Add salt with one or two, set aside and let cool completly.
